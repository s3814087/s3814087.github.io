MYERS-BRIGGS TEST

I believe these results will influence my behaviour in the group in a positive light as i will be able to clearly communicate with my team members, discuss ways to allocate the work evenly amongst yourselves and ensure everyone is on track to complete the task in a timely manner. VARK QUESTIONNAIRE

To me the Vark Questionnaire test results suggest that it is easiest for me to learn new information when its done in more hands on and practical environment such as learning to code through trial and error as opposed to learning from lecture slides. I believe this will influence my behaviour in the team in a positive light as a group project is the practical application of the lessons we learn in class to achieve a real life outcome which the test suggests is my preferred learning method. When forming a team I should look to form one with others who have a similar learning style as this will allow us to work off each other in a way that will improve our performance since we are utilising the same methods to complete the task. I should also seek to avoid forming a team with those whose preferred learning method is the opposite which may create conflict.
